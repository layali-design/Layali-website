— Our Story

Where the day unfolds in golden light.

Layali — Arabic for nights — was born from a simple wish: a place where the rituals of coffee, conversation, and unhurried meals could meet under one roof. We source our beans from single-estate farms across East Africa and Yemen, roasted weekly in small batches just outside Nairobi.

Our kitchen is fully halal, our pastries baked from dawn, and our hospitality rooted in the Arabic tradition of karam — the kind of generosity that turns guests into regulars, and regulars into family.
