Our Story

Where the day unfolds in golden light.

Nova means new in Latin — a new star, a new beginning. We thought of it as the spark of something fresh in Nairobi: a place where mornings unfold slowly, evenings linger long after the last cup, and a table becomes the warmest room in the city.

Our beans come from single-estate farms in Ethiopia, Kenya, and Yemen, roasted weekly in small batches just outside Nairobi. The kitchen is fully halal. The pastries are baked from dawn. And the welcome here is the kind we grew up with.
