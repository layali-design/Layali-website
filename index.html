Our Story

A place to slow down.

Nova Cafe is a modern halal coffee shop in South C, Nairobi, a few minutes from the College of Insurance. Specialty coffee, brunch, fresh pastries, and smoothies in a calm, work-friendly space. No noise, no rush. Just good coffee and a comfortable place to be.
